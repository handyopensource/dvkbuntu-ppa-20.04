Įsitraukimas į DVKbuntu

The DVKbuntu community consists of a group of individuals who made up one of the greatest teams in the open source community, working on various aspects of the distribution, providing advice and technical support, as well as helping to promote DVKbuntu to a wider audience.

No matter your skill level, getting involved and helping to mold the future of DVKbuntu is easy, and you are more than welcome to help out. The work you do will be seen by millions of people around the world.
